How naming works. AstroBlend reads the FITS header for object identifiers: OBJECT, TARGNAME, PROPOSID, and celestial coordinates. It resolves these against MAST and Simbad to produce clean names like NGC 6720 · Ring Nebula or M16 · Cosmic Cliffs (PID 2739). Files that can't be resolved keep their original identifier.
